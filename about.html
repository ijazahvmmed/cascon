About Cascon

Cascon started because we kept seeing the same gap — ecommerce brands with great products but stores that didn't convert, and content that didn't look the part. We built a two-pillar agency to fix both: Shopify development that's engineered for revenue, and AI media production that looks better than traditional shoots. Based in Coventry and Kochi, we work with brands across India, UK, UAE, and beyond.

How we approach work

We tend to start with a small scope and expand from there. Our preference is to prove value before scaling involvement. We avoid long onboarding processes and try to get into the actual work as quickly as the team allows.

Most of our decisions come from what the data shows, not what sounds good in a meeting. If something doesn't work, we say so. If we don't know, we say that too.

What we avoid

We avoid vanity metrics, unnecessary complexity, and work that exists mainly to justify itself. We don't pitch ideas we wouldn't implement ourselves. We try not to create dependencies.

We also avoid growing faster than our ability to do good work. The team stays small on purpose.

How decisions are made

The person doing the work usually makes the call. We keep layers thin. Reporting happens, but not at the expense of momentum. If a decision can be reversed without much cost, it gets made quickly.

Systems thinking

We think in systems, not campaigns. A campaign ends. A system compounds. Most of the work we do is oriented toward building repeatable loops — acquisition, retention, learning — that continue to function without us.
