What we work on

These are the areas Cascon regularly engages in. Each one describes a type of work and the problem space it typically connects to. Not a menu — more of a map.

Paid Performance

This usually involves campaign structure, budget allocation, and channel selection across paid media. Often focused on acquisition cost, return on ad spend, and testing cadence.

Conversion Work

Typically connected to landing pages, checkout flows, and form behavior. The work tends to sit between analytics and front-end implementation. Often involves A/B testing.

Lifecycle & Retention

This usually involves email and messaging flows. Often focused on onboarding sequences, re-engagement triggers, and reducing churn at specific points in the user journey.

Automation

Typically connected to workflow tools, integrations, and internal process design. This work usually touches ops, marketing, and sometimes product simultaneously.

Analytics & Data

Often focused on tracking setup, reporting systems, and measurement clarity. This work is about making existing data usable, not collecting more of it.

Applied AI

This usually involves integrating AI tools into existing workflows. Typically connected to content generation, data processing, or internal tooling. Practical, not theoretical.

Collaboration Models

We usually work in one of three ways: project-based for defined scopes, retained for ongoing involvement, or embedded for teams that need someone inside the work daily. The model tends to follow the problem, not the other way around.
